About Harker Coding Club

Founded in 2022, Harker Coding Club has grown into a community of passionate coders and engineers.

Our Vision

We envision a world where coding literacy is widespread, because coding is a big factor of today's world.

What We Do

We meet weekly, and discuss our projects and their progress! We also have workshops where we all learn something.

Why You Should Join Us

It doesn't matter if you've never coded before. We all start our coding journey somewhere, so why not here?

Our Impact

Since our beginning, we've helped 22 students make over 40 projects in 10 meetings.
